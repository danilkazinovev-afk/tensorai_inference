Frequently asked questions

How do I connect?

Point any HTTP client at https://api.tensorai.com/v1 and set your key. Chat completions, streaming, and tool calls all work as you'd expect over plain REST.

How does billing work?

Pay per token, metered per request — input, output, and cached reads priced separately as listed above. No minimums, no monthly platform fee. You can set a hard spend cap from the dashboard.

Where does inference run?

On data-center-grade GPUs we resell capacity from. You get competitive rates without standing up your own cluster, and we keep the routing thin so latency stays low.

Can I get a model that isn't listed?

Often, yes. If you need a specific open model or a private deployment, email us — with three engineers, turnaround is usually days, not quarters.

Is there a contract or lock-in?

None. Generate a key, send requests, stop whenever. It's a standard HTTP endpoint, so switching away is just pointing your base URL elsewhere.
